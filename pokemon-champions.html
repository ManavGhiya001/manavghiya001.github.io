About Pokémon Champions

Pokémon Champions is an upcoming turn-based strategy game developed by The Pokémon Works, focusing on competitive PvP battles. Announced during the Pokémon Presents showcase in February 2025, the game will be available on Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android platforms. It integrates familiar mechanics such as Pokémon types, abilities, and moves, and features compatibility with Pokémon Home, allowing players to import their favorite Pokémon from previous titles. The game offers various battle modes, including Ranked, Casual, and Private Battles, catering to both competitive and casual players. Learn more
